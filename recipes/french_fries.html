French Fry

Description

Breathtaking picture of extremely crispy french fries that have been salted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 water or as needed

Cooking Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into French fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water prevent them from turning brown
  2. Heat oil in a heavy saucepan or large skillet to 350 degrees F.
  3. While the oil is heating, sift flour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt into a large bowl. Gradually stir in enough water to make a slightly thick batter that can be drizzled from a spoon.
  4. Drain fries and pat dry; dip into the batter one at a time.
  5. Lower fries carefully into the hot oil in batches to prevent them from clumping together. Fry until golden bown and crispy on all sides, about 7-10 minutes.
  6. Drain fries on a paper towel-lined plate to get rid of the excess oil.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
